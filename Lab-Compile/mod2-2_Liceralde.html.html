Haikyuu

The storyline of Haikyu can be summed up as follows: junior high school student Shoyo Hinata becomes obsessed with volleyball after watching Karasuno High School compete in Nationals on television. Being short himself, Hinata was influenced by Karasuno's small yet skilled wing spiker, referred to in the media as "The Little Giant."
